### 1. EXPENSE CLAIMS - Hardcoded Fake Data (HIGH)

**File:** `activity_expense_claims_manager.xml`

| Line | Hardcoded Text | Problem |
|------|---------------|---------|
| 74 | `android:text="LKR 18,250.00"` | Fake pending total shown before Firestore loads |
| 281 | `android:text="Travel Reimbursement"` | Fake claim item |
| 287 | `android:text="Submitted: June 12 • Pending"` | Fake date/status |
| 294 | `android:text="LKR 5,500"` | Fake amount |
| 330 | `android:text="Client Lunch"` | Fake claim item |
| 336 | `android:text="Submitted: June 10 • Approved"` | Fake date/status |
| 343 | `android:text="LKR 3,200"` | Fake amount |

**Impact:** Users see fake expense claims ("Travel Reimbursement", "Client Lunch") that don't exist in Firestore. The "Recent Claims" section (lines 244-348) contains hardcoded XML cards that are ALWAYS visible regardless of actual data.

**Fix:** Remove the hardcoded `layoutRecentClaims` section with fake claim cards (lines 244-348). The actual claims should only come from Firestore via the RecyclerView. Also remove hardcoded "LKR 18,250.00" from the hero card.
